Free Coverage.
Keep It Alive.

Shark Tank Blog has been free for 5+ years. No paywalls, no corporate backing, no VC money. Just a small team and readers who believe independent coverage matters.

1,200+ supporters so far

We started Shark Tank Blog because we believed every founder's story deserves to be told — whether they got a deal or walked away empty-handed. Five years later, we've covered 500+ pitches across 17 seasons.

We don't have a newsroom. We don't have investors. We have a small team of writers who watch every episode, research every company, and publish stories that millions of readers rely on. That costs real money — hosting, research tools, writers' time.

Ads cover some of it. But they also slow down pages, clutter the reading experience, and come with tracking scripts we'd rather not run. Your support changes that equation. It lets us write more, publish faster, and keep the experience clean for everyone.

Common Questions
Where does my money go?
Directly to writers, hosting, and research tools. We're a small independent team with no corporate overhead. Every dollar goes into making the blog better.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your account at any time and you'll keep access until your current period ends.
Do I get an ad-free experience?
Yes. All supporters get a completely ad-free experience across the entire site. Pages load faster and there's no tracking.
Is this tax-deductible?
Shark Tank Blog is not a registered nonprofit, so contributions are not tax-deductible. Think of it as supporting your favorite independent publication.
