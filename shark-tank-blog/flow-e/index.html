Trade-offs

Pros
Highest perceived value — comparison table makes the upgrade tangible. Social proof + guarantee reduce purchase anxiety. Urgency drives now-or-never decisions.
Cons
Heavy, opinionated page — strong sales energy can feel pushy to existing fans. Mobile reflow: testimonials + comparison consume vertical space before the payment form is visible.
When to use
Best for cold traffic and ad-driven landings where the user hasn't been pre-sold. The page does the convincing AND the converting on one URL.
Differs from Flow C
Flow C also puts messaging on the left, but in a calm/editorial tone. Flow E is explicitly sales-oriented — urgency, comparison, testimonial, guarantee — designed to maximize conversion on a single page.
