White Noise An Electric Storm 1969
Experimental Rock

A prototypical release for experimental pop, electronic production, and strange music in general, White Storm's 1969 album An Electric Storm remains mysterious. The work of U.K. tape music innovator Delia Derbyshire and other members of the BBC Radiophonic Workshop, White Storm had few peers at the time, with the United States of America being a similar artist only at times. Interesting pop songs heavy on tape experiments and rudimentary early electronics make this a must-hear for anyone already a fan of the legions of acts that took influence from it.

- Fred Thomas
