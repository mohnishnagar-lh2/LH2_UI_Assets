Takeaway (mock): Variant B's exclusivity framing ("Insider", "24h early") outperforms charity framing by ~47% on click-through, even though the underlying offer ($1/mo, ad-free, newsletter) is identical. Future tests: try urgency ("Limited time"), social proof ("12,431 supporters"), and dollar-anchoring ("Save $24/year vs $3/mo").

Trade-offs

When to use
Whenever you're guessing at copy. CTR is cheap to measure; conversion takes longer.
Watch out for
CTR can lift while paid-conversion drops if the new copy attracts curious clickers but doesn't deliver.
Tech
Cookie-based bucket assignment, GA4 events variant_assigned, banner_click, subscribe_complete. Sticky per-user.
